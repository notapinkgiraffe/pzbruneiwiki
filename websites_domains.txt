# Brunei Domains

## Tools

1) Check whether a domain is available or not: http://bnnic.bn/find-your-domain

Brunei top-level domains (TLDs) supported: 

* .com.bn  
* .edu.bn  
* .org.bn  
* .net.bn

2) Check who owns a current domain (WHOIS): http://bnnic.bn/whois
